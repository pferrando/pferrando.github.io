About Me

I studied Mathematics and Physics at the University of Barcelona, and I am currently studying for a master's degree in Statistics and Operations Research at the Polytechnic University of Catalonia (UPC). My passion is to extract knowledge from the data I analyze and make decisions accordingly to improve results. For this reason, I am especially interested in machine learning.
In recent years I have worked as a digital analytics consultant, analyzing large amounts of data generated in web pages to help our customers with the optimization of their digital strategy and the improvement in the user experience. During these years, I have participated in projects for large clients and I also have worked on designing and developing solutions based on advanced statistical techniques and machine learning.
